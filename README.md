# 🎓 Classroom Comeback Coach

An AI-powered teaching assistant built using AWS PartyRock that helps teachers and tutors generate complete lesson support materials instantly.

## 🚀 Live App
👉 https://partyrock.aws/u/tayraps/2FH1SHqMo/Classroom-Comeback-Coach

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Classroom Comeback Coach is an AI education tool designed to help teachers and tutors quickly turn any CAPS curriculum topic into a structured lesson.

It generates:
- Simple explanations
- Step-by-step solutions (Maths & Sciences)
- Real-life South African examples
- Quiz and exam-style questions
- Teacher tips

---

## 🎯 Features

### 📚 Multi-Subject Support
Supports CAPS subjects including:
- Mathematics & Mathematical Literacy
- Physical Sciences & Natural Sciences
- Life Sciences
- English & Afrikaans
- Geography & History
- Economics, EMS, Accounting, Business Studies

---

### ⚡ Modes
- **Teach Me Mode** → Focus on understanding and learning
- **Exam Mode** → Focus on revision and test preparation

---

### 🧠 Smart Output Structure
Each response includes:
1. Explanation of concept  
2. Worked examples / breakdown  
3. Real-life application (South African context)  
4. Common mistakes  
5. Quick assessment (questions + answers)  
6. Teacher tips  

---

## 💡 Problem Solved
Teachers often spend too much time creating lesson materials and simplifying complex topics. This tool reduces that workload by generating structured, ready-to-use lesson content in seconds.
